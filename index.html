About TM Dynamics

Founded in 2005, TM DYNAMICS SDN. BHD. (TMD) has established itself as one of the Malaysia’s leading importer, wholesaler and exporter in automotive OEM & AM parts for the continental passenger & commercial vehicles segment.

Today, we have grown into a modern corporate group, the sole agent and distributor for various reputable internationally renowned products.



OUR PHILOSOPHY


Our business philosophy is searching for EXCELLENCE. Always being innovative to focus on quality products and providing better services for the perpetuation of corporate growth.

OUR SUCCESS


Much of TMD’s group of companies’ successes are managed by highly experience and professional qualified managers with well-respected and hold excellent reputation in the trade.

OUR BUSINESS NETWORK


In line with the current trend towards globalisation, TMD’s group of companies have local ,regional markets knowledge, well connected network and have made significant inroads into Asia Pacific region.
